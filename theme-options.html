AURA HQ · World-Class Typography · 3 Options

Fraunces (editorial authority + warmth) · Inter pure scale (hyperscaler system) · Syne (geometric brand identity). Each across HQ, Creation, Workspace.

1 · Fraunces + Inter Fraunces (optical-size variable serif) for structural authority positions. Inter for all operational copy. Fraunces is used by Stripe's design team, Loom, and top-tier editorial products. Its optical-size axis means it looks completely different at 26px vs 12px — monumental at display, refined at text. Inter delivers uncompromising UI precision. Together: human authority + machine precision.

2 · Inter Only — Extreme Scale No serif. Inter with a three-tier weight cascade: 800 for structural authority, 600 for UI, 400 for reading. Scale: 26px / 17px / 13px / 9px — large gaps, no middle ground. This is Linear, Vercel, Stripe Docs. The coldness is structural. The warmth comes only from gold and sand. Nothing decorative. Nothing softened.

3 · Syne + Inter Syne for brand/structural positions. Inter for all operational copy. Syne is a geometric display typeface with a distinctive visual signature — wide optical balance, modern weight, used by high-end creative tech and brand-forward products. It reads as unmistakably intentional. No serif — the brand personality comes from Syne's geometry. The most visually distinctive and ownable of the three.
